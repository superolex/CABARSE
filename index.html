Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami.
It was serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump from March 2018 to September 2024,
with its chapters collected in 28 tankōbon volumes as of October 2024.
